FastPing – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

FastPing (“we”, “our”, “the application”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

FastPing does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All processing happens locally on your device. No information is sent to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

To function correctly, FastPing may temporarily access:

a. Network Information (Local Only)

Ping/latency

Network routing path

Connection stability info

This data never leaves your device.

b. System Information (Local Only)

Operating system version

Network adapter type

Local device performance metrics

This information is accessed only for optimization.
We do not log, store, upload, or transmit it.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

FastPing does NOT collect:

Names

Emails

IP addresses

Device identifiers

Account information

Minecraft credentials

Hardware serial numbers

Location data

Payment data

Browsing activity

Cookies or trackers

FastPing does not use analytics, advertising, or cloud services.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Information accessed by FastPing is used only for:

Network optimization

Reducing ping

Stabilizing routes

All data stays on your device and is cleared when the app closes.

4. Third-Party Services

FastPing does not use:

External servers

Analytics providers

Cloud logging

Advertising services

Your data never leaves your system.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer data to:

Individuals

Companies

Advertisers

Government entities

Since we collect nothing, we have nothing to share.

6. Security

FastPing operates entirely offline.
No user data is transmitted over the internet.
This local-only design inherently protects your privacy.

7. Children’s Privacy

FastPing does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed.
The latest version will always appear on our GitHub repository or official website.
