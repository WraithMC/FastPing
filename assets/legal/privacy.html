Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FastPing collects, stores, and uses information.

Information We Collect

FastPing does not collect personal information such as names, addresses, payment data, or identity documents. The website may collect standard non-personal analytics (browser type, device type, page visits).

Software Data

The FastPing software does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal information
  • IP addresses
  • Minecraft credentials
  • Gameplay data
  • Files or system information

FastPing runs locally on your device and does not send data to any server.

Cookies & Web Tracking

The website may use basic analytics (e.g., GitHub Pages, Vercel, Cloudflare) to measure traffic. No personal identification is stored.

Third-Party Services

We link to GitHub, Discord, and other external platforms which have their own privacy policies.

Security

We do not store personal data. Any non-personal analytics collected through the website is protected by the hosting provider’s infrastructure.

Children’s Privacy

FastPing is safe for all ages and does not collect age-identifying data.

Policy Updates

If this Policy changes, updates will be posted publicly on the website.
